Overview

Betanode is a platform for student-computer enthusiasts to learn from one another. For the school, it creates a talent pipeline for students in 9th, 10th and 11th. It is a series of tech workshops conducted over the course of two days.

Goals

  • Enable students to master required skills by giving them a kickstart in those areas.
  • Create a pool of talented students in Computer Science, Design, Audio and Web-Development to benefit the school.

Details

Betanode is a two day in-school event. It is a series of workshops that the students of 9th, 10th and 11th are welcome to attend. These workshops are focused on 3 major learning tracks:

  • Digital Design
  • Web Development
  • Computer Science and Programming

Along with these we also have two other workshops. Students are free to chose the workshops they want to attend.

List of Workshops

How to? Cryptic Hunts Prakhar Mittal & Jainil Ajmera
Cryptic hunts require rigorous mental training provided by this workshop, your skills will be sharpened and tested. Making you competition ready so that you are able to win consistently.

Regardless of being able to play an instrument, with software like FL Studio everyone is able to make music. Music creation is a skill that takes years to hone, yet this workshop will give you a solid foundation to build on.
Get Started with Motion Graphics Shubhankar Das or Rahul Batra
Competency in motion graphics enables one to make eye-catching and brilliant animations. Through Adobe After effects you’ll learn how to bring your thoughts into the real world and create anything you want.
