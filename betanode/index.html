What is Betanode?

Betanode is the brainchild of the presidents of NODE club, Nishant Tripathi and Shubhankar Singh, Rahul Batra, and our alumni, Navoneel Bera, Paymanshu Sharma, Ishan Amlekar.

It is platform where budding digital enthusiasts come together and share their talents and get to become a part of the club, to further hone their skills.
It is an excellent opportunity for future NODE members to gain further insight into their fields.

Why would I do this?

Betanode provides a platform for students which simulates the experience of a computer symposium.

It enables students to showcase their creativity and skills.
