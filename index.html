What is node ?

Node, the technology club of Delhi Public School, Sector 45, Gurgaon, is reputed for its commitment to honing digital skills among young enthusiasts. At node, we believe that our future is what governs our today; and our passion is what drives us to our tomorrow.

Today, node has 100+ computer enthusiasts, from classes VI - XII, each excelling in specific fields like programming, machine learning, digital imaging, motion graphics, photography, video editing, app development, web development, quiz and robotics.

Alphanode is the annual tech symposium conducted by node, and is widely considered to be among the most prestigious events in the Delhi - NCR tech circuit.
